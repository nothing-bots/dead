VC Music Player Commands

  • /play - Play a song via YouTube/URL/query
  • /pause - Pause the music
  • /resume - Resume playback
  • /skip - Skip current track
  • /stop - End voice chat music
  • /playlist - Show group or personal playlist
  • /shuffle - Shuffle current playlist
  • /volume - Adjust volume level
  • /seek - Jump to a timestamp
